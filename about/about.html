About the Project

This website is the result of our final project for junior year U.S. History class. Our instructions were to pick a decade and study the turning points within that decade, through the lens of things like pop culture, politics, and more. To keep things interesting, we were allowed to do it in the form of either a blog or a podcast, and created our own websites using Google Sites. However, because I have coding knoweldge, I decided to code the website entirely by hand.
